Register and Login

I am confused about which category to select?

We can explain the categories and simplify this for you. click here to download a document with detailed explaination of each category.

I have forgotten my password/registered email id

  • Once you register with us, we send you an email, on the registered email-id, containing your registered email-id & password (along with OTP).Kindly look up your mail box for such mail. If not, you can send us a mail from the HELP section, mentioning your registered name, password, country and contact number and after verifying the same, we shall revert to the mail giving you your registered email-id.
  • In case of forgot password, simply click on forgot password and re-type your registered email-id. We will send you a link on that email-id, using which you can reset your password!

I am typing the correct ID and password but not being able to login!

Since you cannot login to go to the HELP section, we suggest you to send us an email at uom@admissiondesk.org from your registered ID explaining the problem and we shall revert back within 24 working hours.

I am not getting OTP on my email address

We suggest you to send us an email at uom@admissiondesk.org with subject - NO OTP, from your registered ID explaining the problem and we shall revert back within 12 working hours.
