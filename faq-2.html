Process

Apply - College/Course

Can I apply for a bachelors/masters course ?

  • To apply for a bachelor's course, you require minimum 12 years of education and a minimum of 6 subjects in higher secondary school qualification
  • A Master degree has a pre-requisite which is successful completion of a bachelor's degree / having completed graduation

Can I apply to more than 1 college ?

Yes! Infact we allow students to select upto 10 colleges per course applied,in the order of their prefernece and based on merit, provide admission in their preferred college.

Can I apply for more than one course?

Ofcourse yes.You can apply for as many courses as you have interest in.However, you will have to apply for each course's eligibility separately(and thus also pay separately for each course)

Can I study in open and distance learning course ?

Well, University does not offer distance learning programs to international students.So that's not an option for you at all.

I want to change the college that has been allocated to me

We offer you to chose your top 10 preferred colleges and assume that you would be happy to have got admission in one of them.Once collge has been allocated based on your preference and the college's merit equirements(NRI cut off).We shall appreciate that you do not request change in the allocated colleges, since they were all your choices eventually.

I want a college in my locality / near my house

Kindly go to the option of search colleges by pin-code and see all colleges around that locality.

I cannot find a college I want to apply to

Kindly send us an email with the subject "ADD COLLEGE" and list down details like the name of the college, course, website, contact and any more as available with you.We shall get in touch with them and ask them to register with us.The process might take a week, however, you can apply to the remaining meanwhile and once the college registers, we shall help you to add that in your preferences.

I cannot find a course I want to apply for

Kindly send us an email with the subject "ADD COURSE" and list down details like the name of the course, colleges offering the same and any more as available with you.We shall get in touch with the colleges and ask them to register the course with us.The process might take a week unless the course cannot be offered by the University.

Can I directly apply with the college

This single window admission process is mandatory for all International Students (coming from a country / board outside India.The consequences of not abiding by the mandate could be === .In case the college accepts your admission directly, you may be held up before examinations and your result shall not be made available to you.

Can I change the course ?

In order to change the course you should have an eligibility letter for your desired course.Thus you're expected to begin with aplying for eligibility immediately before admissions for the year close else you will be requested to apply for a new course in the next academic year.

I had selected one college but my application shows some other

Kindly send us an email from your registered email id & we will get it sorted.

Is there a chance that I'm allocated a college outside my preference list ?

Allocation happens when the students college preference meets the college's percentage requirement(cut-off).Our aim is to provide student's first preference.However, in case any/all the selected preferences do not approve the student's percentage for admission, then we shall step in and provide a college outside the preference list to ensure that the student does not waste an entire academic year.

Can I edit my preferences ?

You are given this option twice during the admission process. Firstly, While selecting the course you can input your preferred college. Secondly, before paying the 2nd application fee you can review your preferences.

Eligibility

I applied long back,still have not heard back from eligibility.When will I get my eligibility letter?

Kindly check the messageBox first to know if there is any unfulfilled requirement with respect to some information, documents or the AIU equivalence certificate(check FAQ for AIU).If there is no additional requirement, then you shall obtain your eligibility letter within 15-25 days of applying.You will be able to downlaod it under MY APPLICATIONS as soon as department accepts your application and considers you eligible.We appreciate your cooperation and patience. If it has already exceeded 30days since you made payment/rectification,as the case may be, then please write to us in the messageBox

I have got my eligibility letter, what do I have to do after this ?

After obtaining your eligibility letter, follow the steps as seen under MY APPLICATION.You may be required to upload student visa depending on your category and then make the second payment towards one time application fee.Upon receipt of this payment,your application will be processed and you will be allocated a college from your preference list depending upon merit.

Am I eligible to apply at UOM?

In order to apply for higher education, you must have the necessary qualification to be eligible.When we say necessary qualification, we mean for a bachelor's course you should have completed higher secondary school(10+2 / 9+3 / 8+4 / O + AS + A level /any other as equivalent to HSC in India with minimum 4 subjects) and for a master's degree you should have passed your bachelor's course.

what is prima facie letter?

Prima facie letter refers to the student's provisional eligibility letter based on which he can move further to pursue his desired course and even to apply for visa.
